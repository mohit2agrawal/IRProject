Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 6: Brahmā Satisfies Lord Śiva

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.6.11

nānāmala-prasravaṇair

nānā-kandara-sānubhiḥ

ramaṇaḿ viharantīnāḿ

ramaṇaiḥ siddha-yoṣitām

SYNONYMS

nānā — various; amala — transparent; prasravaṇaiḥ — with waterfalls; nānā — various; kandara — caves; sānubhiḥ — with summits; ramaṇam — giving pleasure; viharantīnām — sporting; ramaṇaiḥ — with their lovers; siddha-yoṣitām — of the damsels of the mystics.

TRANSLATION

There are many waterfalls, and in the mountains there are many beautiful caves in which the very beautiful wives of the mystics are found.
