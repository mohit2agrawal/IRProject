Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 6: Brahmā Satisfies Lord Śiva

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.6.16

svarṇārṇa-śata-patraiś ca

vara-reṇuka-jātibhiḥ

kubjakair mallikābhiś ca

mādhavībhiś ca maṇḍitam

SYNONYMS

svarṇārṇa — golden colored; śata-patraiḥ — with lotuses; ca — and; vara-reṇuka-jātibhiḥ — with varas, reṇukas and mālatīs; kubjakaiḥ — with kubjakas; mallikābhiḥ — with mallikās; ca — and; mādhavībhiḥ — with mādhavīs; ca — and; maṇḍitam — decorated.

TRANSLATION

There are other trees also which decorate the hill, such as the golden lotus flower, the cinnamon tree, mālatī, kubja, mallikā and mādhavī.
