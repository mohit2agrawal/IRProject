Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 10: Dhruva Mahārāja's Fight With the Yakṣas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.10.7

tato niṣkramya balina

upadeva-mahā-bhaṭāḥ

asahantas tan-ninādam

abhipetur udāyudhāḥ

SYNONYMS

tataḥ — thereafter; niṣkramya — coming out; balinaḥ — very powerful; upadeva — of Kuvera; mahā-bhaṭāḥ — great soldiers; asahantaḥ — unable to tolerate; tat — of the conchshell; ninādam — sound; abhipetuḥ — attacked; udāyudhāḥ — equipped with various weapons.

TRANSLATION

O hero Vidura, the greatly powerful heroes of the Yakṣas, unable to tolerate the resounding vibration of the conchshell of Dhruva Mahārāja, came forth from their city with weapons and attacked Dhruva.
