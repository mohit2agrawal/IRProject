Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 2: Dakṣa Curses Lord Śiva

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.2.5

tatra praviṣṭam ṛṣayo

dṛṣṭvārkam iva rociṣā

bhrājamānaḿ vitimiraḿ

kurvantaḿ tan mahat sadaḥ

SYNONYMS

tatra — there; praviṣṭam — having entered; ṛṣayaḥ — the sages; dṛṣṭvā — seeing; arkam — the sun; iva — just like; rociṣā — with luster; bhrājamānam — shining; vitimiram — free from darkness; kurvantam — making; tat — that; mahat — great; sadaḥ — assembly.

TRANSLATION

When Dakṣa, the leader of the Prajāpatis, entered that assembly, his personal bodily luster as bright as the effulgence of the sun, the entire assembly was illuminated, and all the assembled personalities became insignificant in his presence.
