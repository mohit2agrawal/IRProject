Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 11: Summary Description of the Mahāpuruṣa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.11.43

tvaṣṭā ṛcīka-tanayaḥ

kambalaś ca tilottamā

brahmāpeto 'tha satajid

dhṛtarāṣṭra iṣam-bharāḥ

SYNONYMS

tvaṣṭā — Tvaṣṭā; ṛcīka-tanayaḥ — the son of Ṛcīka (Jamadagni); kambalaḥ — Kambala; ca — and; tilottamā — Tilottamā; brahmāpetaḥ — Brahmāpeta; atha — and; śatajit — Śatajit; dhṛtarāṣṭraḥ — Dhṛtarāṣṭra; iṣam-bharāḥ — the maintainers of the month Iṣa (Āśvina).

TRANSLATION

Tvaṣṭā as the sun-god; Jamadagni, the son of Ṛcīka, as the sage; Kambalāśva as the Nāga; Tilottamā as the Apsarā; Brahmāpeta as the Rākṣasa; Śatajit as the Yakṣa; and Dhṛtarāṣṭra as the Gandharva maintain the month of Iṣa.
