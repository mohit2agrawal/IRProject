Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 12: The Topics of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam Summarized

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.12.63

ṛco yajūḿṣi sāmāni

dvijo 'dhītyānuvindate

madhu-kulyā ghṛta-kulyāḥ

payaḥ-kulyāś ca tat phalam

SYNONYMS

ṛcaḥ — the mantras of the Ṛg Veda; yajūḿṣi — those of the Yajur Veda; sāmāni — and those of the Sāma Veda; dvijaḥ — a brāhmaṇa; adhītya — studying; anuvindate — obtains; madhu-kulyāḥ — rivers of honey; ghṛta-kulyāḥ — rivers of ghee; payaḥ-kulyāḥ — rivers of milk; ca — and; tat — that; phalam — fruit.

TRANSLATION

By studying this Bhāgavatam, a brāhmaṇa can enjoy the same rivers of honey, ghee and milk he enjoys by studying the hymns of the Ṛg, Yajur and Sāma Vedas.
