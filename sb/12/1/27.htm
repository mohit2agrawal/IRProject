Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 1: The Degraded Dynasties of Kali-yuga

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.1.27

saptābhīrā āvabhṛtyā

daśa gardabhino nṛpāḥ

kańkāḥ ṣoḍaśa bhū-pālā

bhaviṣyanty ati-lolupāḥ

SYNONYMS

sapta — seven; ābhīrāḥ — Ābhīras; āvabhṛtyāḥ — of the city of Avabhṛti; daśa — ten; gardabhinaḥ — Gardabhīs; nṛpāḥ — kings; kańkāḥ — Kańkas; ṣoḍaśa — sixteen; bhū-pālāḥ — rulers of the earth; bhaviṣyanti — will be; ati-lolupāḥ — very greedy.

TRANSLATION

Then will follow seven kings of the Ābhīra race from the city of Avabhṛti, and then ten Gardabhīs. After them, sixteen kings of the Kańkas will rule and will be known for their excessive greed.
