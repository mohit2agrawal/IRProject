Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 6: Mahārāja Parīkṣit Passes Away

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.6.79

laugākṣir māńgaliḥ kulyaḥ

kuśīdaḥ kukṣir eva ca

pauṣyañji-siṣyā jagṛhuḥ

saḿhitās te śataḿ śatam

SYNONYMS

laugākṣiḥ māńgaliḥ kulyaḥ — Laugākṣi, Māńgali and Kulya; kuśīdaḥ kukṣiḥ — Kuśīda and Kukṣi; eva — indeed; ca — also; pauṣyañji-śiṣyāḥ — disciples of Pauṣyañji; jagṛhuḥ — they took; saḿhitāḥ — collections; te — they; śatam śatam — each one hundred.

TRANSLATION

Five other disciples of Pauṣyañji, namely Laugākṣi, Māńgali, Kulya, Kuśīda and Kukṣi, each received one hundred saḿhitās.
