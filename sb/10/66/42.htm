Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 66: Pauṇḍraka, the False Vasudeva

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.66.42

dagdhvā vārāṇasīḿ sarvāḿ

viṣṇoś cakraḿ sudarśanam

bhūyaḥ pārśvam upātiṣṭhat

kṛṣṇasyākliṣṭa-karmaṇaḥ

SYNONYMS

dagdhvā — having burned; vārāṇasīm — Vārāṇasī; sarvām — all; viṣṇoḥ — of Lord Viṣṇu; cakram — the disc; sudarśanam — Sudarśana; bhūyaḥ — once again; pārśvam — the side; upātiṣṭhat — went to; kṛṣṇasya — of Kṛṣṇa; akliṣṭa — without trouble or fatigue; karmaṇaḥ — whose actions.

TRANSLATION

After burning down the entire city of Vārāṇasī, Lord Viṣṇu's Sudarśana cakra returned to the side of Śrī Kṛṣṇa, whose actions are effortless.
