Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 63: Lord Kṛṣṇa Fights with Bāṇāsura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.63.8

kumbhāṇḍa-kūpakarṇābhyāḿ

balena saha saḿyugaḥ

sāmbasya bāṇa-putreṇa

bāṇena saha sātyakeḥ

SYNONYMS

kumbhāṇḍa-kūpakarṇābhyām — by Kumbhāṇḍa and Kūpakarṇa; balena saha — with Lord Balarāma; saḿyugaḥ — a fight; sāmbasya — of Sāmba; bāṇa-putreṇa — with the son of Bāṇa; bāṇena saha — with Bāṇa; sātyakeḥ — of Sātyaki.

TRANSLATION

Lord Balarāma fought with Kumbhāṇda and Kūpakarṇa, Sāmba with Bāṇa's son, and Sātyaki with Bāṇa.
