Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 53: Kṛṣṇa Kidnaps Rukmiṇī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.53.14

evaḿ cedi-patī rājā

damaghoṣaḥ sutāya vai

kārayām āsa mantra-jñaiḥ

sarvam abhyudayocitam

SYNONYMS

evam — in the same way; cedi-patiḥ — the lord of Cedi; rājā damaghoṣaḥ — King Damaghoṣa; sutāya — for his son (Śiśupāla); vai — indeed; kārayām āsa — had done; mantra-jñaiḥ — by expert knowers of mantras; sarvam — everything; abhyudaya — to his prosperity; ucitam — conducive.

TRANSLATION

Rājā Damaghoṣa, lord of Cedi, had also engaged brāhmaṇas expert in chanting mantras to perform all rituals necessary to assure his son's prosperity.
