Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 53: Kṛṣṇa Kidnaps Rukmiṇī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.53.1

śrī-śuka uvāca

vaidarbhyāḥ sa tu sandeśaḿ

niśamya yadu-nandanaḥ

pragṛhya pāṇinā pāṇiḿ

prahasann idam abravīt

SYNONYMS

śrī-śukaḥ uvāca — Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; vaidarbhyāḥ — of the princess of Vidarbha; saḥ — He; tu — and; sandeśam — the confidential message; niśamya — hearing; yadu-nandanaḥ — Lord Kṛṣṇa, the descendant of Yadu; pragṛhya — taking; pāṇinā — by His hand; pāṇim — the hand (of the brāhmaṇa messenger); prahasan — smiling; idam — this; abravīt — said.

TRANSLATION

Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: Thus hearing the confidential message of Princess Vaidarbhī, Lord Yadunandana took the brāhmaṇa's hand and, smiling, spoke to him as follows.
