Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 58: Kṛṣṇa Marries Five Princesses

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.58.11

yudhiṣṭhira uvāca

kiḿ na ācaritaḿ śreyo

na vedāham adhīśvara

yogeśvarāṇāḿ durdarśo

yan no dṛṣṭaḥ ku-medhasām

SYNONYMS

yudhiṣṭhiraḥ uvāca — Yudhiṣṭhira said; kim — what; naḥ — by us; ācaritam — performed; śreyaḥ — pious work; na veda — do not know; aham — I; adhīśvara — O supreme controller; yoga — of mystic yoga; īśvarāṇām — by the masters; durdarśaḥ — rarely seen; yat — that; naḥ — by us; dṛṣṭaḥ — seen; ku-medhasam — who are unintelligent.

TRANSLATION

King Yudhiṣṭhira said: O supreme controller, I do not know what pious deeds we fools have done so that we can see You, whom the masters of yogic perfection rarely see.
