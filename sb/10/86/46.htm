Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 86: Arjuna Kidnaps Subhadrā, and Kṛṣṇa Blesses His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.86.46

śṛṇvatāḿ gadatāḿ śaśvad

arcatāḿ tvābhivandatām

ṇṛṇāḿ saḿvadatām antar

hṛdi bhāsy amalātmanām

SYNONYMS

śṛṇvatām — for those who are hearing; gadatām — speaking; śaśvat — constantly; arcatām — worshiping; tvā — You; abhivandatām — offering praise; nṛṇām — for men; saḿvadatām — conversing; antaḥ — within; hṛdi — the heart; bhāsi — You appear; amala — spotless; ātmanām — whose minds.

TRANSLATION

You reveal Yourself within the hearts of those persons of pure consciousness who constantly hear about You, chant about You, worship You, glorify You and converse with one another about You.
