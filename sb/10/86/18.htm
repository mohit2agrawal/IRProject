Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 86: Arjuna Kidnaps Subhadrā, and Kṛṣṇa Blesses His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.86.18

nārado vāmadevo 'triḥ

kṛṣṇo rāmo 'sito 'ruṇiḥ

ahaḿ bṛhaspatiḥ kaṇvo

maitreyaś cyavanādayaḥ

SYNONYMS

nāradaḥ vāmadevaḥ atriḥ — the sages Nārada, Vāmadeva and Atri; kṛṣṇaḥ — Kṛṣṇa-dvaipāyana Vyāsa; rāmaḥ — Lord Paraśurāma; asitaḥ aruṇiḥ — Asita and Aruṇi; aham — I (Śukadeva); bṛhaspatiḥ kaṇvaḥ — Bṛhaspati and Kaṇva; maitreyaḥ — Maitreya; cyavana — Cyavana; ādayaḥ — and others.

TRANSLATION

Among these sages were Nārada, Vāmadeva, Atri, Kṛṣṇa-dvaipāyana Vyāsa, Paraśurāma, Asita, Aruṇi, myself, Bṛhaspati, Kaṇva, Maitreya and Cyavana.
