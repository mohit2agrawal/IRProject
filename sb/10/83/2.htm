Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 83: Draupadī Meets the Queens of Kṛṣṇa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.83.2

ta evaḿ loka-nāthena

paripṛṣṭāḥ su-sat-kṛtāḥ

pratyūcur hṛṣṭa-manasas

tat-pādekṣā-hatāḿhasaḥ

SYNONYMS

te — they (Yudhiṣṭhira and Lord Kṛṣṇa's other relatives); evam — thus; loka — of the universe; nāthena — by the Lord; paripṛṣṭāḥ — questioned; su — very much; sat-kṛtāḥ — honored; pratyūcuḥ — replied; hṛṣṭa — joyful; manasaḥ — whose minds; tat — His; pāda — the feet; īkṣā — by seeing; hata — destroyed; aḿhasaḥ — whose sins.

TRANSLATION

Feeling greatly honored, King Yudhiṣṭhira and the others, freed of all sinful reactions by seeing the feet of the Lord of the universe, gladly answered His inquiries.
