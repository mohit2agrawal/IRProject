Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 89: Kṛṣṇa and Arjuna Retrieve a Brāhmaṇa's Sons

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.89.25

evaḿ dvitīyaḿ viprarṣis

tṛtīyaḿ tv evam eva ca

visṛjya sa nṛpa-dvāri

tāḿ gāthāḿ samagāyata

SYNONYMS

evam — in the same manner; dvitīyam — a second time; vipra-ṛṣiḥ — the wise brāhmaṇa; tṛtīyam — a third time; tu — and; evam eva ca — just the same way; visṛjya — leaving (his dead son); saḥ — he; nṛpa-dvāri — at the King's door; tām — the same; gāthām — song; samagāyata — he sang.

TRANSLATION

The wise brāhmaṇa suffered the same tragedy with his second and third child. Each time, he left the body of his dead son at the King's door and sang the same song of lamentation.
