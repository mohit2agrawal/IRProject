Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 17: The History of Kāliya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.17.18

dehi dānaḿ dvi-jātīnāḿ

kṛṣṇa-nirmukti-hetave

nandaḥ prīta-manā rājan

gāḥ suvarṇaḿ tadādiśat

SYNONYMS

dehi — you should give; dānam — charity; dvi-jātīnām — to the brāhmaṇas; kṛṣṇa-nirmukti — the safety of Kṛṣṇa; hetave — for the purpose of; nandaḥ — Nanda Mahārāja; prīta-manāḥ — satisfied within his mind; rājan — O King Parīkṣit; gāḥ — cows; suvarṇam — gold; tadā — then; ādiśat — gave.

TRANSLATION

The brāhmaṇas then advised Nanda Mahārāja, "To assure that your son Kṛṣṇa will always be free from danger, you should give charity to the brāhmaṇas." With a satisfied mind, O King, Nanda Mahārāja then very gladly gave them gifts of cows and gold.
