Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 41: Kṛṣṇa and Balarāma Enter Mathurā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.41.3

tam apṛcchad dhṛṣīkeśaḥ

kiḿ te dṛṣṭam ivādbhutam

bhūmau viyati toye vā

tathā tvāḿ lakṣayāmahe

SYNONYMS

tam — of him; apṛcchat — asked; hṛṣīkśaḥ — Lord Kṛṣṇa; kim — whether; te — by you; dṛṣṭam — seen; iva — indeed; adbhutam — something exceptional; bhūmau — on the earth; viyati — in the sky; toye — in the water; va — or; tathā — so; tvām — you; lakṣayāmahe — We surmise.

TRANSLATION

Lord Kṛṣṇa asked Akrūra: Have you seen something wonderful on the earth, in the sky or in the water? From your appearance, We think you have.
