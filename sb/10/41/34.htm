Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 41: Kṛṣṇa and Balarāma Enter Mathurā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.41.34

sa yācito bhagavatā

paripūrṇena sarvataḥ

sākṣepaḿ ruṣitaḥ prāha

bhṛtyo rājñaḥ su-durmadaḥ

SYNONYMS

saḥ — he; yācitaḥ — requested; bhagavatā — by the Supreme Lord; paripūrṇena — who is absolutely complete; sarvataḥ — in all respects; sa-ākṣepam — insultingly; ruṣitaḥ — angered; prāha — he spoke; bhṛtyaḥ — the servant; rājñaḥ — of the King; su — very much; durmadaḥ — falsely proud.

TRANSLATION

Thus requested by the Supreme Lord, who is perfectly complete in all respects, that arrogant servant of the King became angry and replied insultingly.
