Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 36: The Slaying of Arista, the Bull Demon

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.36.35

tataś caiṣā mahī mitra

bhavitrī naṣṭa-kaṇṭakā

SYNONYMS

tataḥ — then; ca — and; eṣā — this; mahī — earth; mitra — O friend; bhavitrī — will be; naṣṭa — destroyed; kaṇṭakā — her thorns.

TRANSLATION

Then, my friend, this earth will be free of thorns.
