Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 55: The History of Pradyumna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.55.17

sa ca śambaram abhyetya

saḿyugāya samāhvayat

aviṣahyais tam ākṣepaiḥ

kṣipan sañjanayan kalim

SYNONYMS

saḥ — He; ca — and; śambaram — Śambara; abhyetya — approaching; saḿyugāya — to battle; samāhvayat — called him; aviṣahyaiḥ — intolerable; tam — him; ākṣepaiḥ — with insults; kṣipan — reviling; sañjanayan — inciting; kalim — a fight.

TRANSLATION

Pradyumna approached Śambara and called him to battle, hurling intolerable insults at him to foment a conflict.
