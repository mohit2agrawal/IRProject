Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 43: Kṛṣṇa Kills the Elephant Kuvalayāpīḍa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.43.29

vadanty anena vaḿśo 'yaḿ

yadoḥ su-bahu-viśrutaḥ

śriyaḿ yaśo mahatvaḿ ca

lapsyate parirakṣitaḥ

SYNONYMS

vadanti — they say; anena — by Him; vaḿśaḥ — the dynasty; ayam — this; yadoḥ — descending from King Yadu; su-bahu — very much; viśrutaḥ — famous; śriyam — riches; yaśaḥ — glory; mahatvam — power; ca — and; lapsyate — it will achieve; parirakṣitaḥ — protected on all sides.

TRANSLATION

It is said that under His full protection the Yadu dynasty will become extremely famous and attain wealth, glory and power.
