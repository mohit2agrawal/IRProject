Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 43: Kṛṣṇa Kills the Elephant Kuvalayāpīḍa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.43.35

tasmād rājñaḥ priyaḿ yūyaḿ

vayaḿ ca karavāma he

bhūtāni naḥ prasīdanti

sarva-bhūta-mayo nṛpaḥ

SYNONYMS

tasmāt — therefore; rājñaḥ — the King's; priyam — pleasure; yūyam — You two; vayam — we; ca — also; karavāma he — let us do; bhūtāni — all living beings; naḥ — with us; prasīdanti — will be satisfied; sarva-bhūta — all beings; mayaḥ — comprising; nṛpaḥ — the king.

TRANSLATION

Therefore let's do what the King wants. Everyone will be pleased with us, for the king embodies all living beings.
