Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 57: Satrājit Murdered, the Jewel Returned

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.57.11

so 'pi kṛtodyamaḿ jñātvā

bhītaḥ prāṇa-parīpsayā

sāhāyye kṛtavarmāṇam

ayācata sa cābravīt

SYNONYMS

saḥ — he (Śatadhanvā); api — also; kṛta-udyamam — preparing Himself; jñātvā — learning; bhītaḥ — frightened; prāṇa — his life air; parīpsayā — wishing to save; sāhāyye — for assistance; kṛtavarmāṇam — Kṛtavarmā; ayācata — he entreated; saḥ — he; ca — and; abravīt — said.

TRANSLATION

Upon learning that Lord Kṛṣṇa was preparing to kill him, Śatadhanvā was struck with fear. To save his life he approached Kṛtavarmā and begged him for help, but Kṛtavarmā replied as follows.
