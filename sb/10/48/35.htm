Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 48: Kṛṣṇa Pleases His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.48.35

gaccha jānīhi tad-vṛttam

adhunā sādhv asādhu vā

vijñāya tad vidhāsyāmo

yathā śaḿ suhṛdāḿ bhavet

SYNONYMS

gaccha — go; jānīhi — learn; tat — his (Dhṛtarāṣṭra's); vṛttam — activity; adhunā — at present; sādhu — good; asādhu — evil; vā — or; vijñāya — knowing; tat — that; vidhāsyāmaḥ — We will arrange; yathā — so that; śam — the benefit; suhṛdām — of Our dear ones; bhavet — will be.

TRANSLATION

Go and see whether Dhṛtarāṣṭra is acting properly or not. When We find out, We will make the necessary arrangements to help Our dear friends.
