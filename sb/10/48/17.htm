Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 48: Kṛṣṇa Pleases His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.48.17

diṣṭyā pāpo hataḥ kaḿsaḥ

sānugo vām idaḿ kulam

bhavadbhyām uddhṛtaḿ kṛcchrād

durantāc ca samedhitam

SYNONYMS

diṣṭyā — by good fortune; pāpaḥ — sinful; hataḥ — killed; kaḿsaḥ — Kaḿsa; sa-anugaḥ — together with his brothers and other followers; vām — of Yours; idam — this; kulam — dynasty; bhavadbhyām — by You two; uddhṛtam — delivered; kṛcchrāt — from difficulty; durantāt — endless; ca — and; samedhitam — made prosperous.

TRANSLATION

[Akrūra said:] It is our good fortune that You two Lords have killed the evil Kaḿsa and his followers, thus delivering Your dynasty from endless suffering and causing it to flourish.
