Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 78: The Killing of Dantavakra, Vidūratha and Romaharṣaṇa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.78.39

taḿ pāpaḿ jahi dāśārha

tan naḥ śuśrūṣaṇaḿ param

pūya-śoṇita-vin-mūtra-

surā-māḿsābhivarṣiṇam

SYNONYMS

tam — that; pāpam — sinful person; jahi — please kill; dāśārha — O descendant of Daśārha; tat — that; naḥ — to us; śuśrūṣaṇam — service; param — best; pūya — pus; śoṇita — blood; vit — feces; mūtra — urine; surā — wine; māḿsa — and meat; abhivarṣiṇam — who pours down.

TRANSLATION

O descendant of Daśārha, please kill that sinful demon, who pours down pus, blood, feces, urine, wine and meat upon us. This is the best service You can do for us.
