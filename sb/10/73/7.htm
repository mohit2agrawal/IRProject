Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 73: Lord Kṛṣṇa Blesses the Liberated Kings

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.73.7

kṛṣṇa-sandarśanāhlāda

dhvasta-saḿrodhana-klamāḥ

praśaśaḿsur hṛṣīkeśaḿ

gīrbhiḥ prāñjalayo nṛpāḥ

SYNONYMS

kṛṣṇa-sandarśana — of seeing Lord Kṛṣṇa; āhlāda — by the ecstasy; dhvasta — eradicated; saḿrodhana — of imprisonment; klamāḥ — whose weariness; praśaśaḿsuḥ — they praised; hṛṣīkā-īśam — the supreme master of the senses; gīrbhiḥ — with their words; prāñjalayaḥ — with joined palms; nṛpāḥ — the kings.

TRANSLATION

The ecstasy of beholding Lord Kṛṣṇa having dispelled the weariness of their imprisonment, the kings stood with joined palms and offered words of praise to that supreme master of the senses.
