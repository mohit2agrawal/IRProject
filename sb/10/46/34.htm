Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 46: Uddhava Visits Vṛndāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.46.34

āgamiṣyaty adīrgheṇa

kālena vrajam acyutaḥ

priyaḿ vidhāsyate pitror

bhagavān sātvatāḿ patiḥ

SYNONYMS

āgamiṣyati — He will return; adīrgheṇa — not long; kālena — in time; vrajam — to Vraja; acyutaḥ — Kṛṣṇa, the infallible one; priyam — satisfaction; vidhāsyate — He will give; pitroḥ — to His parents; bhagavān — the Supreme Lord; sātvatām — of the devotees; patiḥ — master and protector.

TRANSLATION

Infallible Kṛṣṇa, the Lord of the devotees, will soon return to Vraja to satisfy His parents.

PURPORT

Here Uddhava begins to deliver Lord Kṛṣṇa's message.
