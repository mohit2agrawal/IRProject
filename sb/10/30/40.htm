Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 30: The Gopīs Search for Kṛṣṇa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.30.40

śrī-śuka uvāca

anvicchantyo bhagavato

mārgaḿ gopyo 'vidūritaḥ

dadṛśuḥ priya-viśleṣān

mohitāḿ duḥkhitāḿ sakhīm

SYNONYMS

śrī-śukaḥ uvāca — Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; anvicchantyaḥ — searching out; bhagavataḥ — of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; mārgam — the path; gopyaḥ — the gopīs; avidūritaḥ — not far away; dadṛśuḥ — saw; priya — from Her beloved; viśleṣāt — because of the separation; mohitām — bewildered; duḥkhitām — unhappy; sakhīm — their friend.

TRANSLATION

Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: While continuing to search out Kṛṣṇa's path, the gopīs discovered their unhappy friend close by. She was bewildered by separation from Her lover.
