Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 82: Kṛṣṇa and Balarāma Meet the Inhabitants of Vṛndāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.82.21

kaḿsa-pratāpitāḥ sarve

vayaḿ yātā diśaḿ diśam

etarhy eva punaḥ sthānaḿ

daivenāsāditāḥ svasaḥ

SYNONYMS

kaḿsa — by Kaḿsa; pratāpitāḥ — severely troubled; sarve — all; vayam — we; yātāḥ — went away; diśam diśam — in various directions; etarhi eva — just now; punaḥ — again; sthānam — to our proper places; daivena — by Providence; āsāditāḥ — brought; svasaḥ — O sister.

TRANSLATION

Harassed by Kaḿsa, we all fled in various directions, but by the grace of Providence we have now finally been able to return to our homes, my dear sister.
