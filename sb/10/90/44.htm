Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 90: Summary of Lord Kṛṣṇa's Glories

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.90.44

tan-nigrahāya hariṇā

proktā devā yadoḥ kule

avatīrṇāḥ kula-śataḿ

teṣām ekādhikaḿ nṛpa

SYNONYMS

tat — of them; nigrahāya — for the subduing; hariṇā — by Lord Kṛṣṇa; proktāḥ — told; devāḥ — the demigods; yadoḥ — of Yadu; kule — in the family; avatīrṇāḥ — descended; kula — of clans; śatam — one hundred; teṣām — their; eka-adhikam — plus one; nṛpa — O King (Parīkṣit).

TRANSLATION

To subdue these demons, Lord Hari told the demigods to descend into the dynasty of Yadu. They comprised 101 clans, O King.
