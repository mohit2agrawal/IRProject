Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 76: The Battle Between Śālva and the Vṛṣṇis

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.76.3

śālvaḥ pratijñām akaroc

chṛṇvatāḿ sarva-bhūbhujām

ayādavāḿ kṣmāḿ kariṣye

pauruṣaḿ mama paśyata

SYNONYMS

śālvaḥ — Śālva; pratijñām — a promise; akarot — made; śṛṇvatām — as they listened; sarva — all; bhū-bhujām — the kings; ayādavām — devoid of Yādavas; kṣmām — the earth; kariṣye — I will make; pauruṣam — prowess; mama — my; paśyata — just see.

TRANSLATION

Śālva swore in the presence of all the kings: "I will rid the earth of Yādavas. Just see my prowess!"
