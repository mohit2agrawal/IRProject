Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 14: Lord Kṛṣṇa Explains the Yoga System to Śrī Uddhava

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.14.35

evaḿ praṇava-saḿyuktaḿ

prāṇam eva samabhyaset

daśa-kṛtvas tri-ṣavaṇaḿ

māsād arvāg jitānilaḥ

SYNONYMS

evam — thus; praṇava — with the syllable oḿ; saḿyuktam — joined; prāṇam — the prāṇāyāma system of controlling the bodily airs; eva — indeed; samabhyaset — one should carefully practice; daśa-kṛtvaḥ — ten times; tri-savanam — at sunrise, noon and sunset; māsāt — one month; arvāk — after; jita — one will conquer; anilaḥ — the life air.

TRANSLATION

Being fixed in the oḿkāra, one should carefully practice the prāṇāyāma system ten times at each sunrise, noon and sunset. Thus, after one month one will have conquered the life air.
