Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 30: The Disappearance of the Yadu Dynasty

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.30.20

śareṣu hīyamāeṣu

bhajyamānesu dhanvasu

śastreṣu kṣīyamāneṣu

muṣṭibhir jahrur erakāḥ

SYNONYMS

śareṣu — the arrows; hīyamāneṣu — as they became depleted; bhajyamāneṣu — as they became broken; dhanvasu — the bows; śastreṣu — the missile weapons; kṣīyamāneṣu — as they were used up; muṣṭibhiḥ — with their fists; jahruḥ — they seized; erakāḥ — cane stalks.

TRANSLATION

When all their bows had been broken and their arrows and other missiles spent, they seized the tall stalks of cane with their bare hands.
