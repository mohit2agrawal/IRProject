Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 2: Mahārāja Nimi Meets the Nine Yogendras

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.2.15

priyavrato nāma suto

manoḥ svāyambhuvasya yaḥ

tasyāgnīdhras tato nābhir

ṛṣabhas tat-sutaḥ smṛtaḥ

SYNONYMS

priyavrataḥ — Mahārāja Priyavrata; nāma — by name; sutaḥ — the son; manoḥ svāyambhuvasya — of Svāyambhuva Manu; yaḥ — who; tasya — his; āgnīdhraḥ — (son was) Āgnīdhra; tataḥ — from him (Āgnīdhra); nābhiḥ — King Nābhi; ṛṣabhaḥ — Lord Ṛṣabhadeva; tat-sutaḥ — his son; smṛtaḥ — is so remembered.

TRANSLATION

Svāyambhuva Manu had a son named Mahārāja Priyavrata, and among Priyavrata's sons was Āgnīdhra. From Āgnīdhra was born Nābhi, whose son was known as Ṛṣabhadeva.

PURPORT

The genealogical background of the sons of Ṛṣabhadeva is given in this verse.
