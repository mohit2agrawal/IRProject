Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 18: Diti Vows to Kill King Indra

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.18.16

anuhrādasya sūryāyāḿ

bāṣkalo mahiṣas tathā

virocanas tu prāhrādir

devyāḿ tasyābhavad baliḥ

SYNONYMS

anuhrādasya — of Anuhlāda; sūryāyām — through Sūryā; bāṣkalaḥ — Bāṣkala; mahiṣaḥ — Mahiṣa; tathā — also; virocanaḥ — Virocana; tu — indeed; prāhrādiḥ — the son of Prahlāda; devyām — through his wife; tasya — of him; abhavat — was; baliḥ — Bali.

TRANSLATION

The wife of Anuhlāda was named Sūryā. She gave birth to two sons, named Bāṣkala and Mahiṣa. Prahlāda had one son, Virocana, whose wife gave birth to Bali Mahārāja.
