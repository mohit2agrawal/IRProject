Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 3: Yamarāja Instructs His Messengers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.3.3

śrī-śuka uvāca

bhagavat-puruṣai rājan

yāmyāḥ pratihatodyamāḥ

patiḿ vijñāpayām āsur

yamaḿ saḿyamanī-patim

SYNONYMS

śrī-śukaḥ uvāca — Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; bhagavat-puruṣaiḥ — by the order carriers of the Lord, the Viṣṇudūtas; rājan — O King; yāmyāḥ — the order carriers of Yamarāja; pratihata-udyamāḥ — whose efforts were defeated; patim — their master; vijñāpayām āsuḥ — informed; yamam — Yamarāja; saḿyamanī-patim — the master of the city Saḿyamanī.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī replied: My dear King, when the order carriers of Yamarāja were baffled and defeated by the order carriers of Viṣṇu, they approached their master, the controller of Saḿyamanī-purī and master of sinful persons, to tell him of this incident.
