Canto 1: CreationChapter 10: Departure of Lord Kṛṣṇa for Dvārakā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 1.10.18

uddhavaḥ sātyakiś caiva

vyajane paramādbhute

vikīryamāṇaḥ kusumai

reje madhu-patiḥ pathi

SYNONYMS

uddhavaḥ — a cousin-brother of Kṛṣṇa's; sātyakiḥ — His driver; ca — and; eva — certainly; vyajane — engaged in fanning; parama-adbhute — decorative; vikīryamāṇaḥ — seated on scattered; kusumaiḥ — flowers all around; reje — commanded; madhu-patiḥ — the master of Madhu (Kṛṣṇa); pathi — on the road.

TRANSLATION

Uddhava and Sātyaki began to fan the Lord with decorated fans, and the Lord, as the master of Madhu, seated on scattered flowers, commanded them along the road.
