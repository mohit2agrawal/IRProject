Canto 1: CreationChapter 14: The Disappearance of Lord Kṛṣṇa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 1.14.24

vilokyodvigna-hṛdayo

vicchāyam anujaḿ nṛpaḥ

pṛcchati sma suhṛn madhye

saḿsmaran nāraderitam

SYNONYMS

vilokya — by seeing; udvigna — anxious; hṛdayaḥ — heart; vicchāyam — pale appearance; anujam — Arjuna; nṛpaḥ — the King; pṛcchati sma — asked; suhṛt — friends; madhye — amongst; saḿsmaran — remembering; nārada — Sage Nārada; īritam — indicated by.

TRANSLATION

Seeing Arjuna pale due to heartfelt anxieties, the King, remembering the indications of the sage Nārada, questioned him in the midst of friends.
