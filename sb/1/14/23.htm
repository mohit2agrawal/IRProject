Canto 1: CreationChapter 14: The Disappearance of Lord Kṛṣṇa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 1.14.23

taḿ pādayor nipatitam

ayathā-pūrvam āturam

adho-vadanam ab-bindūn

sṛjantaḿ nayanābjayoḥ

SYNONYMS

tam — him (Arjuna); pādayoḥ — at the feet; nipatitam — bowing down; ayathā-pūrvam — unprecedented; āturam — dejected; adhaḥ-vadanam — downward face; ap-bindūn — drops of water; sṛjantam — creating; nayana-abjayoḥ — from the lotuslike eyes.

TRANSLATION

When he bowed at his feet, the King saw that his dejection was unprecedented. His head was down, and tears glided from his lotus eyes.
