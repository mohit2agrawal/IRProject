Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 13: Description of Future Manus

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.13.18

navamo dakṣa-sāvarṇir

manur varuṇa-sambhavaḥ

bhūtaketur dīptaketur

ity ādyās tat-sutā nṛpa

SYNONYMS

navamaḥ — ninth; dakṣa-sāvarṇiḥ — Dakṣa-sāvarṇi; manuḥ — the Manu; varuṇa-sambhavaḥ — born as the son of Varuṇa; bhūtaketuḥ — Bhūtaketu; dīptaketuḥ — Dīptaketu; iti — thus; ādyāḥ — and so on; tat — his; sutāḥ — sons; nṛpa — O King.

TRANSLATION

O King, the ninth Manu will be Dakṣa-sāvarṇi, who is born of Varuṇa. Among his sons will be Bhūtaketu, and Dīptaketu.
