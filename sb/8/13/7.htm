Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 13: Description of Future Manus

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.13.7

sańkṣepato mayoktāni

sapta-manvantarāṇi te

bhaviṣyāṇy atha vakṣyāmi

viṣṇoḥ śaktyānvitāni ca

SYNONYMS

sańkṣepataḥ — in brief; mayā — by me; uktāni — explained; sapta — seven; manu-antarāṇi — changes of Manu; te — unto you; bhaviṣyāṇi — the future Manus; atha — also; vakṣyāmi — I shall speak; viṣṇoḥ — of Lord Viṣṇu; śaktyā anvitāni — empowered by the energy; ca — also.

TRANSLATION

I have briefly explained to you the position of the seven Manus. Now I shall describe the future Manus, along with the incarnations of Lord Viṣṇu.
