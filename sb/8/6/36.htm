Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 6: The Demigods and Demons Declare a Truce

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.6.36

tāḿs tathā bhagna-manaso

bhagna-bāhūru-kandharān

vijñāya bhagavāḿs tatra

babhūva garuḍa-dhvajaḥ

SYNONYMS

tān — all the demigods and demons; tathā — thereafter; bhagna-manasaḥ — being brokenhearted; bhagna-bāhu — with broken arms; ūru — thighs; kandharān — and shoulders; vijñāya — knowing; bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Viṣṇu; tatra — there; babhūva — appeared; garuḍa-dhvajaḥ — being carried on Garuḍa.

TRANSLATION

The demigods and demons were frustrated and disheartened, and their arms, thighs and shoulders were broken. Therefore the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who knows everything, appeared there on the back of His carrier, Garuḍa.
