Canto 9: LiberationChapter 2: The Dynasties of the Sons of Manu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.2.19

citraseno nariṣyantād

ṛkṣas tasya suto 'bhavat

tasya mīḍhvāḿs tataḥ pūrṇa

indrasenas tu tat-sutaḥ

SYNONYMS

citrasenaḥ — one named Citrasena; nariṣyantāt — from Nariṣyanta, another son of Manu; ṛkṣaḥ — Ṛkṣa; tasya — of Citrasena; sutaḥ — the son; abhavat — became; tasya — of him (Ṛkṣa); mīḍhvān — Mīḍhvān; tataḥ — from him (Mīḍhvān); pūrṇaḥ — Pūrṇa; indrasenaḥ — Indrasena; tu — but; tat-sutaḥ — the son of him (Pūrṇa).

TRANSLATION

From Nariṣyanta came a son named Citrasena and from him a son named Ṛkṣa. From Ṛkṣa came Mīḍhvān, from Mīḍhvān came Pūrṇa, and from Pūrṇa came Indrasena.
