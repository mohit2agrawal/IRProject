Canto 9: LiberationChapter 13: The Dynasty of Mahārāja Nimi

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.13.24

tasmāt samarathas tasya

sutaḥ satyarathas tataḥ

āsīd upagurus tasmād

upagupto 'gni-sambhavaḥ

SYNONYMS

tasmāt — from Kṣemādhi; samarathaḥ — a son named Samaratha; tasya — from Samaratha; sutaḥ — son; satyarathaḥ — Satyaratha; tataḥ — from him (Satyaratha); āsīt — was born; upaguruḥ — Upaguru; tasmāt — from him; upaguptaḥ — Upagupta; agni-sambhavaḥ — a partial expansion of the demigod Agni.

TRANSLATION

The son of Kṣemādhi was Samaratha, and his son was Satyaratha. The son of Satyaratha was Upaguru, and the son of Upaguru was Upagupta, a partial expansion of the fire-god.
