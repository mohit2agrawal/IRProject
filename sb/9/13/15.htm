Canto 9: LiberationChapter 13: The Dynasty of Mahārāja Nimi

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.13.15

tasmād bṛhadrathas tasya

mahāvīryaḥ sudhṛt-pitā

sudhṛter dhṛṣṭaketur vai

haryaśvo 'tha marus tataḥ

SYNONYMS

tasmāt — from Devarāta; bṛhadrathaḥ — a son named Bṛhadratha; tasya — of him (Bṛhadratha); mahāvīryaḥ — a son named Mahāvīrya; sudhṛt-pitā — he became the father of King Sudhṛti; sudhṛteḥ — from Sudhṛti; dhṛṣṭaketuḥ — a son named Dhṛṣṭaketu; vai — indeed; haryaśvaḥ — his son was Haryaśva; atha — thereafter; maruḥ — Maru; tataḥ — thereafter.

TRANSLATION

From Devarāta came a son named Bṛhadratha and from Bṛhadratha a son named Mahāvīrya, who became the father of Sudhṛti. The son of Sudhṛti was known as Dhṛṣṭaketu, and from Dhṛṣṭaketu came Haryaśva. From Haryaśva came a son named Maru.
