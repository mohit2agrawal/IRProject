Canto 9: LiberationChapter 15: Paraśurāma, the Lord's Warrior Incarnation

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.15.39

vayaḿ hi brāhmaṇās tāta

kṣamayārhaṇatāḿ gatāḥ

yayā loka-gurur devaḥ

pārameṣṭhyam agāt padam

SYNONYMS

vayam — we; hi — indeed; brāhmaṇāḥ — are qualified brāhmaṇas; tāta — O my dear son; kṣamayā — with the quality of forgiveness; arhaṇatām — the position of being worshiped; gatāḥ — we have achieved; yayā — by this qualification; loka-guruḥ — the spiritual master of this universe; devaḥ — Lord Brahmā; pārameṣṭhyam — the supreme person within this universe; agāt — achieved; padam — the position.

TRANSLATION

My dear son, we are all brāhmaṇas and have become worshipable for the people in general because of our quality of forgiveness. It is because of this quality that Lord Brahmā, the supreme spiritual master of this universe, has achieved his post.
