Canto 9: LiberationChapter 23: The Dynasties of the Sons of Yayāti

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.23.2

janamejayas tasya putro

mahāśālo mahāmanāḥ

uśīnaras titikṣuś ca

mahāmanasa ātmajau

SYNONYMS

janamejayaḥ — Janamejaya; tasya — of him (Janamejaya); putraḥ — a son; mahāśālaḥ — Mahāśāla; mahāmanāḥ — (from Mahāśāla) a son named Mahāmanā; uśīnaraḥ — Uśīnara; titikṣuḥ — Titikṣu; ca — and; mahāmanasaḥ — from Mahāmanā; ātmajau — two sons.

TRANSLATION

From Sṛñjaya came a son named Janamejaya. From Janamejaya came Mahāśāla; from Mahāśāla, Mahāmanā; and from Mahāmanā two sons, named Uśīnara and Titikṣu.
