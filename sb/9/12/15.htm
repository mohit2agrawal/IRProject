Canto 9: LiberationChapter 12: The Dynasty of Kuśa, the Son of Lord Rāmacandra

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.12.15

raṇako bhavitā tasmāt

surathas tanayas tataḥ

sumitro nāma niṣṭhānta

ete bārhadbalānvayāḥ

SYNONYMS

raṇakaḥ — Raṇaka; bhavitā — will take birth; tasmāt — from Kṣudraka; surathaḥ — Suratha; tanayaḥ — the son; tataḥ — thereafter; sumitraḥ — Sumitra, the son of Suratha; nāma — by the name; niṣṭha-antaḥ — the end of the dynasty; ete — all the above-mentioned kings; bārhadbala-anvayāḥ — in the dynasty of King Bṛhadbala.

TRANSLATION

From Kṣudraka will come Raṇaka, from Raṇaka will come Suratha, and from Suratha will come Sumitra, ending the dynasty. This is a description of the dynasty of Bṛhadbala.
