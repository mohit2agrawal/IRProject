Canto 9: LiberationChapter 6: The Downfall of Saubhari Muni

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.6.20

purañjayasya putro 'bhūd

anenās tat-sutaḥ pṛthuḥ

viśvagandhis tataś candro

yuvanāśvas tu tat-sutaḥ

SYNONYMS

purañjayasya — of Purañjaya; putraḥ — son; abhūt — was born; anenāḥ — by the name Anenā; tat-sutaḥ — his son; pṛthuḥ — of the name Pṛthu; viśvagandhiḥ — of the name Viśvagandhi; tataḥ — his son; candraḥ — by the name Candra; yuvanāśvaḥ — of the name Yuvanāśva; tu — indeed; tat-sutaḥ — his son.

TRANSLATION

The son of Purañjaya was known as Anenā, Anenā's son was Pṛthu, and Pṛthu's son was Viśvagandhi. Viśvagandhi's son was Candra, and Candra's son was Yuvanāśva.
