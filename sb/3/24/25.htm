Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 24: The Renunciation of Kardama Muni

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.24.25

tatas ta ṛṣayaḥ kṣattaḥ

kṛta-dārā nimantrya tam

prātiṣṭhan nandim āpannāḥ

svaḿ svam āśrama-maṇḍalam

SYNONYMS

tataḥ — then; te — they; ṛṣayaḥ — the sages; kṣattaḥ — O Vidura; kṛta-dārāḥ — thus married; nimantrya — taking leave of; tam — Kardama; prātiṣṭhan — they departed; nandim — joy; āpannāḥ — obtained; svam svam — each to his own; āśrama-maṇḍalam — hermitage.

TRANSLATION

Thus married, the sages took leave of Kardama and departed full of joy, each for his own hermitage, O Vidura.
